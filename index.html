Proving the Concept

🔒

100% Localhost Isolation

Conventional models transmit continuous tracking data to external servers, creating heavy latency. Our framework structurally blocks external pings, executing a strict zero-data-leak protocol directly on the device.

⚡

Soft Probabilistic Engine

Human behavior cannot be confined to rigid temporal bounds. Instead of triggering false errors when you wake up 20 minutes late, the statistical model expands tolerance margins like an elastic band to temporarily absorb temporal drift.

⚙️

Under The Hood

A completely isolated, three-tier ecosystem. A streamlined Frontend hides complex math, the Local Engine executes rule-based shift decisions without cloud dependency, and the Local DB acts as an impenetrable safe for behavioral data.
